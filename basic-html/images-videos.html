Images

Images are the pictures that are shown on a website.
They are made with the image tag alongside a src and alt attribute for the image location and image description.
Images also contain width & height attributes

Figures

Figures used to provide addition information or context to an image or video.
Figures main tags are figure, nested between are an image tag and figcaption tags for the image description.

Videos

Videos are used to display videos on a website.
They are made with the video tag. Unlike images, videos often have a few source tags nested inside.
Video tags hold the controls, autoplay, loop, muted, width & height and a poster attribute to display an image.
The source tags hold the src attribute, along side a type="video/***" to show the video format.

Audio

Audio is used to play music and other sound effects on a website. They work similarly to video tags but are slightly different.
They are made with the audio tag. Like video, they have a few source tags nested inside holding a type="audio/***".
Audio also takes the controls, loop, autoplay and muted attributes.

iFrames

iFrames are used to embed other webpages and external content.
Created using the iframe tags they take the src, width & height attributes along side new frameborder and allowfullscreen.
