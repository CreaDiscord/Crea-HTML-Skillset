Tables & Semantics

Tables

Tables are used to display data on a website.
They are made using a collection of nested tags as followed:

table
Declares a the table and structure.
caption
Adds a title for the table, nested in table.
thead
The head section of the table, nested intsble.
tbody
The body section of the table, nested in table.
tfoot
The footer of the table, nested in table.
tr
Creates the rows in the tables, nested in thead, tbody and tfoot.
th
Creates hraders for your rows/columns, nested in tr.
td
Creates the data in your rows/colums, nested in tbody and tfoot.

th & td both take the colspan & rowspan attribute.
